Upgrades explanation
Your Tornado Tier is the main indication of your progress. Gaining a tier unlocks new parts of the game or give you boosts that otherwise help you progress.
T1ChallengeUnlocks Challenges and the Basic Challenge.
T2AutoOmegaPrestigeUnlocks automation for Omega prestiges.
T3Challenge_drunk
T3Challenge_DrainInternally, you unlock a challenge called "Drain", but this is not yet available.
T4EquationUnlocks Spiral Equations or Equipment.
T5Challenge_Flower
T6AutoPrestigeCompletely useless.
T7Challenge_Slow
T8Enemy_PIUnlocks the fifth enemy, called PI.
T9Challenge_gluttony
T10Z_boolsterUnlocks Z Reactors, which provide boosts based on delta z you "put into them".
T11Challenge_Exponentiation
T12Enemy_GammaUnlocks the sixth enemy, called Gamma, which is the main source of attack speed and variables contributing to delta z.
T13NewSigmaUnlocks the variable m and changes your main formula, which currently gives you a 100% boost to delta r.
T14Challenge_Poor
T15Challenge_noRebirth
T16Enemy_NapierUnlocks the seventh enemy, called Napier.
T17criticalGain 10% crit chance, and double crit damage.
T18Challenge_sinkhole
T19mUnlocks the z upgrade to increase m.
T20Challenge_sphere
T21integralUnlocks the p upgrades alpha and beta.
T22new_battle_upgradeUnlock battle upgrades 26-29.
T23z_reactor_cUnlock the Crit Rate Z Reactor.

T26z_reactor_cxUnlock the SCrit Rate Z Reactor.

T28alhazenUnlock Spiral Equation XIII, which gives Block.
